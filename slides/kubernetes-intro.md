### Kubernetes


#### About Kubernetes

* Greek word for _helmsman_ or _pilot_
* Also origin of words like _cybernetics_ and _government_
* Inspired by _Borg_, Google's internal scheduling tool
* Play on _Borg cube_
* [Source](https://news.ycombinator.com/item?id=9653797)
